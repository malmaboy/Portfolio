Projects

Beira Mar!


Beira Mar! is a 3D serious game, where you have to collect different seashells on an island, so you can complete the collections. This game was specially created for people with cognitive and physical disabilities.

Loctan's Adventures


Loctan's Adventures is a 3D third-person action puzzle game, where you play as Loctan, trying to return to his planet after being forced to leave, in order to protect a powerful relic, a necklace, which in the wrong hands, could cause chaos. Loctan ends up on planet Earth where everything is giant compared to him and has to repair his spaceship to go home.

Redemption: The Deadly Sin


Redemption: The Deadly Sin is a first-person puzzle game, where you played as Detective Jackson, investigating crimes committed by a religious maniac, who killed his daughter through pain during an attempt to educate her on religion. Feeling guilty, he then kept going by torturing those who didn't believe in God, attempting to convert them, even if it meant their death.

Alter


Alter is a first-person puzzle game where you play as Layla, a girl who was kidnapped and taken to medical facilities owned by a shady organization, where she was submitted to multiple experiments, ending up with multiple personalities and a shape-shifting body. Layla has to solve puzzles, that the organization submits her to, while also trying to escape the facility by using her multiple personalities.
